Keyboard Functional Bindings ( * indicates currently implemented : as of 26/11/2011 ) :

ESC* : Exit the program

F1  : Help

F2  : Low level rendering

F3  : Medium level

F4  : High level rendering

F5* : Toggle constellations display

F6* : Toggle pulsars display

F7* : Toggle supernovae display

F8* : Toggle celestial sphere right ascension and declination grid

Consider the screen view as looking out to the front of the spacecraft

Q*  : Stop craft movement

A*  : Forward craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

Z*  : Reverse craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

W*  : Leftwards craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

R*  : Rightwards craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

T*  : Upwards craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

B*  : Downwards craft thrust ( translation of centre of mass )

E*  : Upwards pitch of craft ( rotation about centre of mass )

D*  : Downwards pitch of craft ( rotation about centre of mass )

X*  : Leftwards yaw of craft  ( rotation about centre of mass )

V*  : Rightwards yaw of craft  ( rotation about centre of mass )

S*  : Leftwards roll of craft  ( rotation about centre of mass )

F*  : Rightwards roll of craft  ( rotation about centre of mass )

G*  : go home to initial craft position

NB Current controller model is 'inertial', that is : it will happen in a certain way until you stop it.
   If in doubt hit Q and get your bearings. You will bounce off the Earth and get punted back if you go too far way.
